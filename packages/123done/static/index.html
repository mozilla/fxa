How it works

Jot. Stick. Knock down.

1

Jot a new note

Type a task into the compose bar and hit Add. It lands on the board in the next post-it color.

2

Stick it on the board

Your notes scatter across the grid, each pinned at a friendly hand-placed angle.

3

Knock it down

Click a note when it’s done, and it gets a red strike, a red pin, and a rotated “DONE” stamp.
