About
So what is
all this then?

123done is a very simple todo list application. Jot a task, slap it on the board, and click it to knock it down. That’s the whole trick.

Type a task and add it to your board.

Click a note to mark it done.

Click a finished note again to clear it.

Your task list is stored on your device so you can use it right away. If you want to sync 123done across devices, sign in with your Firefox account, and your notes follow you everywhere.

123done is a demo of Firefox Accounts OAuth: a small relying-party app used to exercise sign-in, subscription, and account flows end to end.
